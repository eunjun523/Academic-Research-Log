# 🎓 Academic Research Portfolio

Welcome! I am **Eunjun**, an undergraduate researcher at **[CVMLab](https://sites.google.com/view/cvmlab/home)**, Dongduk Women's University. 

I am dedicated to advancing **Image Quality Assessment (IQA)** and **AI-Generated Content (AIGC)**. This repository serves as a professional log of my academic journey, paper reviews, and research insights.

## 🎯 Research Interests

### **1. Image Quality Assessment (IQA)**
* **Perceptual Quality:** Understanding how humans perceive quality in digital media.
* **No-Reference IQA:** Developing metrics that don't require a ground-truth reference, specifically for real-world applications.

### **2. AI-Generated Content (AIGC)**
* **Generative Model Evaluation:** Establishing robust evaluation frameworks for Diffusion models and GANs.
* **Artifact Detection:** Identifying and quantifying visual inconsistencies (artifacts) in AI-generated images and videos.
* **Multimodal Assessment:** Evaluating the alignment between text prompts and generated visual content.

### **3. Deep Learning for Vision**
* **CNN & Transformers:** Utilizing state-of-the-art architectures to model complex visual features.
* **Low-level Vision Tasks:** Applying deep learning for image restoration, super-resolution, and enhancement.
